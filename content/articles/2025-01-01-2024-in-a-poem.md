Days passed, seasons turned

Burritos wrapped with practiced hand
A craft I honed, though not so planned
Paths cleared as tutor and student
Roles played well, wouldn't you say?

Games were played with rigs anew
Parts chosen from users of old
YOU DIED flashed a thousand times
I did all right, did I not?

Mixed gins to cheers and tales
Blackjack and poker, fortunes swayed
Vodka gulped to hands of fail
Gamblings tempting, is it not?

Peeps met from places around
Hours walked in the dead of night
Anime talks from fate to citrus
The banter was fun, was it not?

This year was all right, I think.
