The beauty of some gardens lies in the calculated chaos it perfects. Plant growth is substantially unpredictable making it utterly chaotic for the human eye. The beauty of a garden exists in the placement and manipulation of these plants. Which needs the calculation to achieve so as to make it possible to capture the chaotic nature of plants into the domain of artistry. This in turn is a manipulation of chaos as per a calculated basis. This is calculated chaos. For the sole purpose of enjoying. Enslaving chaos in accordance with a pattern.
