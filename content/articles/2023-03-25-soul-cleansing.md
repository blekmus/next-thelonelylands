Have you ever experienced a moment when you feel like you're on top of the world? When your soul feels completely cleansed and refreshed, where nothing else seems to matter, and you're content just being where you are? It's a rare occurrence for most people, but when it does happen, it's like a breath of fresh air for the soul. I've had a few of these moments in my life, and they always leave me feeling like I'm soaring above the clouds.

One such moment for me is when I'm heading home after surviving a social activity. As someone with social anxiety, the relief of finishing an event and heading back to the comfort of my own space is indescribable. It's not that I'm in a rush to leave, but rather the sense of accomplishment and growth that comes with facing my fears and pushing past them. That sense of personal triumph, combined with the feeling of a huge weight being lifted off my shoulders, is what makes the journey home feel like I'm on top of the world.

But there's another source of this soul-cleansing feeling for me, and that's through the media I consume. Bloom Into You and Gintama are two of the most impactful shows that have left me feeling rejuvenated and glad to be alive. There's something about these shows that just lifts my spirits and makes me feel alive. They're like a beacon of light that I can hold when life gets tough. They really know how to tug at my heartstrings and leave me with a renewed sense of hope and joy.

It's funny how something as simple as a TV show or finishing a task can bring us so much happiness. But in a world that can be so overwhelming and exhausting, it's important to cherish those moments of peace and contentment. They remind me of the beauty that exists in the world and give me the strength to keep moving forward. So if you ever find yourself feeling on top of the world, embrace it, savour it, and let it fill your soul. It's these moments that make life truly worth living.
